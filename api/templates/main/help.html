To patient records

You can create and edit new patient records, monitor the patient's condition and monitor the dynamics.

Creating a Patient Record

To create a patient card, first you need to go the patient table click on the "Add patient" button and go to a new page.

On new page,enter the patient's full name, gender and date of birth. Fields "Address" and "Kin activity" are not required to fill out. It is also necessary to indicate the number of the compulsory medical insurance policy. (OMI- obligatory medical insurance) to uniquely identify the patient in the database.

Now, "Save the patient's medical record", a patient record is created in the database and you can see her. To do this, you need to click on the main menu "Patients", select "Patients" in the side menu and click on the "Open" button in the form of a green square with a pen in the line of the patient of interest
